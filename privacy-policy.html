Smart Mirror Policy

1. Purpose

This policy outlines the proper usage, maintenance, and privacy practices for the Smart Mirror. It is designed to ensure the safety, security, and optimal performance of the device.

2. Acceptable Use

The Smart Mirror is intended for personal use. Users must ensure that the device is not used for any unlawful activities, such as:

  • Unauthorized access to other systems
  • Harmful data collection
  • Violation of privacy rights

3. Data Privacy

The Smart Mirror collects certain data to improve user experience. The following guidelines apply to data collection and usage:

  • Personal data will only be stored locally unless explicitly shared by the user.
  • No data will be shared with third parties without user consent.
  • Users can delete their data at any time through the device's settings.

4. Maintenance

To ensure the longevity of the Smart Mirror, users are advised to:

  • Keep the mirror clean and free from dust or moisture.
  • Perform regular software updates to enhance performance and security.
  • Report any hardware or software issues promptly to the support team.

5. Liability

The manufacturer is not liable for any damages caused by unauthorized modifications or improper usage of the Smart Mirror. Users are responsible for ensuring compliance with this policy.
